Budget BrewsTM is a website that crowdsources beer prices in San Luis Obispo, CA. Users can view the cheapest prices for 12-packs and 30-packs for each reported beer and also view their store and the time which that beer price was reported.
Users can create an account then log in, at which point they can subscribe to any beer to receive an email when that beer's price is reported to have changed.
Users who are logged in are also able to up-vote and down-vote posts to verify the validity of a price report. They can also view their profile page in which they will see their "reputation" which is calculated by the number of up-votes that they have received minus the number of down-votes they have received.
